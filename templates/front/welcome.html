A list of your checks, one for each Cron job, daemon or periodically running task you want to monitor.

Configurable Period and Grace Time

Each check has configurable Period and Grace Time parameters.
Depending on these parameters and time since the last ping, the check is in one of the following states:new, up, late, down.

What is Healthchecks?

Easy task scheduling

Create new checks and give them name for easy recognition later. Adjust Period and Grace time to match the periodicity and duration of your tasks.

Instant alerts

Receive instant alerts for whenver a task goes down or goes up when it has been down or runs too often.

Easy periodic reports scheduling

Set periodic time after which to receive a summary of the status of all your scheduled tasks. The reports can be set to be received on a daily, weekly and monthly basis.

Wide range of Integrations

Add a number of ways to receive notifications for example SMS, Slack etc

Easy Team Collaboration

Add team members who can also monitor your tasks.
